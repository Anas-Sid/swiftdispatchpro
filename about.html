Our Story

Built by Truckers, for Truckers

SwiftDispatch Pro was founded in 2015 by former owner-operators who understood the challenges of finding profitable loads and managing the complexities of freight dispatch. What started as a small operation helping local drivers has grown into a nationwide network serving thousands of successful truckers.

Our founders, Maria Rodriguez and James Thompson, combined their 30+ years of trucking experience with cutting-edge technology to create a dispatch service that truly understands the needs of professional drivers.
